ABOUT

WE COME TO YOU!

Quality and Convenient detailing is what Mobile Detail Solutions is all about. Have your vehicles washed and detailed from any location in or around Ottawa. We’ve created our company in a way that allows you to enjoy our professional auto detailing service from the location of your choice.

Mobile Detail Solutions has experience in the vehicle detailing business. We started in Edmonton, Alberta, servicing many areas in Western Canada, which included areas such as Regina, Edmonton, Winnipeg, and Fort Nelson. After many years, we decided to expand into the Ottawa region, bringing our efficient, industry-leading services into the nation’s capital

As the region’s premier vehicle detailing service, we have worked to create a system that protects, cleans, and refurbishes both commercial, and residential vehicles in a manner that is most comfortable and convenient for our clients. Our state-of-the-art, eco-friendly equipment, together with our proven team of professionals will have all vehicles operating just as good as they will look!
